## 🚨 Red Flags to Watch For

When you see these flags, be careful:

- ⚠️ **Unlimited liability** = You could owe unlimited money
- ⚠️ **Automatic renewal** = Renews automatically (hard to cancel)
- ⚠️ **Non-compete** = Can't work with competitors (restricts your career)
- ⚠️ **Unilateral termination** = Other party can quit anytime
- ⚠️ **Indefinite confidentiality** = Forever obligation
- ⚠️ **Arbitration only** = Can't sue in court

## 📚 Using Clause Templates

### Why Templates Matter
Compare what you have vs. what's standard/fair

### How to Use:
1. **Go to Chat**
2. **Ask**: "Show me a standard payment clause"
3. **Compare**: See pro-vendor vs. pro-client versions
4. **Negotiate**: Use templates to argue for better terms

### Example:
**Pro-Client Version:**
```
"Payment due within 45 days. No interest on undisputed invoices."
```

**Pro-Vendor Version:**
```
"Payment due within 15 days. 2% monthly fee on late payments."
```
