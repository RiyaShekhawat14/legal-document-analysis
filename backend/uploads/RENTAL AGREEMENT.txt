RENTAL AGREEMENT

1. Termination Clause
The landlord may terminate this agreement at any time without prior notice if the tenant fails to pay rent on time.

2. Payment Terms
The tenant must pay the monthly rent before the 5th of each month. Late payment will attract a penalty.

3. Security Deposit
The tenant shall provide a security deposit equivalent to two months rent which will be refunded at the end of the lease.

4. Maintenance Responsibility
The tenant is responsible for minor maintenance and repairs.

5. Renewal Clause
This agreement will automatically renew for another 11 months unless terminated in writing by either party.
